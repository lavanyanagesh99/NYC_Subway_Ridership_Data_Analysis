# NYC Subway Ridership Data Analysis

## Overview
This project explores trends in NYC subway ridership from 2020 to 2024, focusing on the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic and recovery patterns. By integrating various datasets, it provides insights into borough-level recovery, subway line popularity, and shifting commuter preferences, with an eye toward predicting future trends.

## Objectives
- Analyze subway ridership trends pre- and post-COVID.
- Understand borough-wise and line-specific recovery patterns.
- Explore alternative transit modes (like CitiBike and Uber).
- Predict future ridership trends using data-driven models.
